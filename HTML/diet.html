Diet Plans

  • Keto Diet

    A low-carb, high-fat diet that has been shown to help with weight loss and improve certain health conditions.

  • Mediterranean Diet

    Inspired by the traditional dietary patterns of countries bordering the Mediterranean Sea, it emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, fish, and healthy fats like olive oil.

  • Vegetarianism & Veganism Diet

    Vegetarian diets exclude meat but may include dairy and eggs, while vegan diets exclude all animal products.

  • Whole30 Diet

    This short-term diet eliminates sugar, alcohol, grains, legumes, soy, and dairy for 30 days, aiming to reset eating habits and identify food sensitivities.

  • Intermittent Diet

    : Rather than focusing on what foods to eat, intermittent fasting involves cycling between periods of eating and fasting.

  • Low-Carb Diet

    These diets restrict carbohydrate intake, which can vary in intensity from moderately low-carb to very low-carb . They often prioritize protein and fat intake.
